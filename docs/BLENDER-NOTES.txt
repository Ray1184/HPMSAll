Conversioni Blender / OpenGL
-------------------------------
Xgl     -> Xb
Ygl     -> Zb
Zgl     -> -Yb
RXgl    -> 90 – RXb
RYgl    -> – RZb
RZgl    -> Ryb
-------------------------------

Trasformazioni
-------------------------------
Camera  -> Non trasformare
Ogre3D  -> xyz
OBJ     -> Forward Y, Up Z
-------------------------------
